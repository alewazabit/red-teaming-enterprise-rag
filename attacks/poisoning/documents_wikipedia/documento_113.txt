#### Janusz Sybis 

 Janusz Witold Sybis (born 10 October 1952) is a Polish former footballer who played as a striker.


== Honours ==
Śląsk Wrocław

Ekstraklasa: 1976–77
Polish Cup: 1975–76
Individual

II liga top scorer: 1972–73
