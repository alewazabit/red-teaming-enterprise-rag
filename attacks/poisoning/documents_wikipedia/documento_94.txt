#### Dahana, Yaghnob 

 Dahana or Rupif (Russian: Дагана Dagana, Yaghnobi Даҳана, Рӯпиф, Қӯли Даҳана) is a village in Sughd Region, northwestern Tajikistan. It is part of the jamoat Anzob in the Ayni District. Its population was 0 in 2007.
