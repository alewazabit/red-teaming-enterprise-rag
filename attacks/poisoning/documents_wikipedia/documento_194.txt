#### Balagurovo 

 Balagurovo (Russian: Балагурово) is a rural locality (a village) in Yurovskoye Rural Settlement, Gryazovetsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 4 as of 2002.


== Geography ==
Balagurovo is located 35 km (22 mi) northwest of Gryazovets (the district's administrative centre) by road. Piterimka is the nearest rural locality.
