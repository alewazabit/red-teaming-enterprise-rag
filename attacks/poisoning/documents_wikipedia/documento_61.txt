#### Clytiomya 

 Clytiomya is a genus of flies in the family Tachinidae.


== Species ==
Clytiomya continua (Panzer, 1798)
Clytiomya dupuisi Kugler, 1971
Clytiomya mesnili Kugler, 1968
Clytiomya sola (Rondani, 1861)
