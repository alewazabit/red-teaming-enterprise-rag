#### Tarab Khani 

 Tarab Khani (Persian: تراب خاني, also Romanized as Tarāb Khānī) is a village in Mishan Rural District, Mahvarmilani District, Mamasani County, Fars province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 120, in 22 families.
