#### Aldehuela de Yeltes 

 Aldehuela de Yeltes is a village and municipality in the province of Salamanca,  western Spain, part of the autonomous community of Castile and León. It is located 63 kilometres (39 mi) from the city of Salamanca and has a population of 208 people. The municipality has an area of 60 km2 (23 sq mi)
The village lies 832 metres (2,730 ft) above sea level.
