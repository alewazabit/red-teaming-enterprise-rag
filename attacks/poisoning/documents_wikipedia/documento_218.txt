#### Chengbei Subdistrict, Linxia City 

 Chengbei Subdistrict (Chinese: 城北街道; pinyin: Chéngběi Jiēdào; lit. 'city north') is a subdistrict in the heart of Linxia City, Gansu, People's Republic of China. As of 2011, it has six residential communities (居委会) under its administration.
