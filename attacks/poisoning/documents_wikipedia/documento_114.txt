#### Rougemontot 

 Rougemontot (French pronunciation: [ʁuʒmɔ̃to]) is a commune in the Doubs département in the Bourgogne-Franche-Comté region in eastern France.


== Geography ==
Rougemontot lies 18 km (11 mi) northeast of Marchaux.
